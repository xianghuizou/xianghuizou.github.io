Chelsea Football Club

London, England

The history of CFC

Chelsea Football Club was founded on March 10th, 1905. The london club was not well-known until 1955, when they won their first domestic league title. Chelsea made their first European debut in 1957 but did not make a promising start. Chelsea is becoming more and more famous by the end of the 20th century, when they started being a regular force in European football competitions, winning the first UEFA Super Cup, beating Real Madrid 1-0. 50 years after Chelsea's first domestic league title, Chelsea won their second league title (the Barclay's Premier league) in 2005 and successfully defended the league title in 2006. Finally in 2012, Chelsea won the heaviest title in the club history, the UEFA Championship league, beating Bayern Munich 4-3 in the penalty shoot-out.
