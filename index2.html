Summary

Lisound Shenyang was originally founded in Fuzhou, China. Our family started this family business in 1997 and has since then owned all Lisound branches in Liaoning, China. There are five Lisound branches in Liaoning, including four in Shenyang and one in Benxi.

Product introduction

Xiandi 12TR Model

This is the newest model launched to the market in 2014. With 6 cores and 12 channels in such a tiny machine, this brand new model can process sounds that almost resembles human ear functions. The speed of sound processing is twice as fast as the old Sofia model and dampens noise levels by at least 10dB.
